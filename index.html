The Psychic Game

You have 9 tries to guess what letter I am thinking of.
Any letter in the alphabet A-Z. No special characters, no tricky magic.
If you manage to crack the code, you are awarded a point and the computer resets and you get to prove yourself again.
If you do not guess correctly, your guess will be recorded and you may try again. If you are unable to decipher the code in 9 tries, you have lost. The code will reset.
